<!-- WHAT THIS IS: -->
<!-- The post-project review (PIR) is a structured learning exercise that -->
<!-- every project must complete before it can be formally closed. -->
<!-- -->
<!-- This is not a blame exercise. It is not a performance review. It is -->
<!-- the mechanism by which the organisation gets better at running -->
<!-- projects with each cycle — converting experience into institutional -->
<!-- knowledge rather than letting it disappear when the team disperses. -->
<!-- -->
<!-- The automated governance check blocks project archiving until this -->
<!-- review is completed, closed, and its improvement suggestions -->
<!-- submitted to the governance improvement process. -->
<!-- -->
<!-- A review that produces no improvement suggestions is a review that -->
<!-- found nothing to learn. That is possible — but it must be stated -->
<!-- explicitly, not left blank. -->
